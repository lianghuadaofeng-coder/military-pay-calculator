How Much Does a Staff Sergeant / Petty Officer First Class (E-6) Make in 2026?

Updated 2026-06-08

In 2026 a Staff Sergeant / Petty Officer First Class (E-6) earns between $3,401 and $5,268 per month in basic pay, depending on years of service — plus tax-free BAH and BAS that raise real take-home pay further.

E-6 is a senior non-commissioned officer (NCO) grade, usually reached after 6–10 years.

E-6 pay beyond basic pay

Basic pay is only part of the paycheck. Most E-6s also receive tax-free BAH (housing — varies by ZIP code and dependents) and BAS ($476.95/month (enlisted)). Because allowances are not taxed, a E-6's real take-home pay is noticeably higher than the basic-pay figure alone.

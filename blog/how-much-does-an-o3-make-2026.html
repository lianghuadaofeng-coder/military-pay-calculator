How Much Does a Captain / Lieutenant (O-3) Make in 2026?

Updated 2026-06-08

In 2026 a Captain / Lieutenant (O-3) earns between $5,535 and $9,004 per month in basic pay, depending on years of service — plus tax-free BAH and BAS that raise real take-home pay further.

O-3 (Army/Air Force/Marine Captain or Navy Lieutenant) is the rank most company-grade officers hold for several years.

O-3 pay beyond basic pay

Basic pay is only part of the paycheck. Most O-3s also receive tax-free BAH (housing — varies by ZIP code and dependents) and BAS ($328.48/month (officer)). Because allowances are not taxed, a O-3's real take-home pay is noticeably higher than the basic-pay figure alone.

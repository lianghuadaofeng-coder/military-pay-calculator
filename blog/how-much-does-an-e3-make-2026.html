How Much Does a Private First Class / Lance Corporal / Airman First Class (E-3) Make in 2026?

Updated 2026-06-08

In 2026 a Private First Class / Lance Corporal / Airman First Class (E-3) earns between $2,837 and $3,198 per month in basic pay, depending on years of service — plus tax-free BAH and BAS that raise real take-home pay further.

E-3 is typically reached within the first year or two of service.

An E-3 with 2 years of service earns $3,015/month in basic pay (about $36,180/year) before allowances.

E-3 pay beyond basic pay

Basic pay is only part of the paycheck. Most E-3s also receive tax-free BAH (housing — varies by ZIP code and dependents) and BAS ($476.95/month (enlisted)). Because allowances are not taxed, a E-3's real take-home pay is noticeably higher than the basic-pay figure alone.

How Much Does a Major / Lieutenant Commander (O-4) Make in 2026?

Updated 2026-06-08

In 2026 a Major / Lieutenant Commander (O-4) earns between $6,294 and $10,510 per month in basic pay, depending on years of service — plus tax-free BAH and BAS that raise real take-home pay further.

O-4 is a field-grade officer rank, typically reached around the 10-year mark.

O-4 pay beyond basic pay

Basic pay is only part of the paycheck. Most O-4s also receive tax-free BAH (housing — varies by ZIP code and dependents) and BAS ($328.48/month (officer)). Because allowances are not taxed, a O-4's real take-home pay is noticeably higher than the basic-pay figure alone.

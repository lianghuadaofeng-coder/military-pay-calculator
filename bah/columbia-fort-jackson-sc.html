How BAH works in Columbia / Fort Jackson, SC

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Columbia / Fort Jackson, SC Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Columbia / Fort Jackson, SC's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

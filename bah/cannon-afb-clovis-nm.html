How BAH works in Cannon AFB / Clovis, NM

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Cannon AFB / Clovis, NM Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Cannon AFB / Clovis, NM's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

How BAH works in Reno / Carson City, NV

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Reno / Carson City, NV Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Reno / Carson City, NV's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

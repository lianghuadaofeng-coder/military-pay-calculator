Updated 2026-06-08 · Official 2025 DoD rates

How BAH works in San Bernardino, CA

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the San Bernardino, CA Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, San Bernardino, CA's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

These are the official 2025 rates (the latest complete public dataset). 2026 BAH rose about 4.2% on average nationwide; for an exact current figure, enter your ZIP in the military pay calculator or check the DoD BAH lookup. Learn more in our 2026 BAH guide.

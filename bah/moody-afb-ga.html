How BAH works in Moody AFB, GA

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Moody AFB, GA Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Moody AFB, GA's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

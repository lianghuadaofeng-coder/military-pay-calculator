How BAH works in Essex CO, MA

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Essex CO, MA Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Essex CO, MA's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

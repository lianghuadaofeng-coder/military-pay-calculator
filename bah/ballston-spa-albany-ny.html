How BAH works in Ballston Spa / Albany, NY

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Ballston Spa / Albany, NY Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Ballston Spa / Albany, NY's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

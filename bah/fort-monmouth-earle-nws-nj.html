How BAH works in Fort Monmouth / Earle NWS, NJ

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Fort Monmouth / Earle NWS, NJ Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Fort Monmouth / Earle NWS, NJ's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

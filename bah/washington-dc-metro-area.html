Washington, DC Metro Area BAH Rates (2025)

Updated 2026-06-08 · Official 2025 DoD rates

How BAH works in Washington, DC Metro Area

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Washington, DC Metro Area Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Washington, DC Metro Area's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

These are the official 2025 rates (the latest complete public dataset). 2026 BAH rose about 4.2% on average nationwide; for an exact current figure, enter your ZIP in the military pay calculator or check the DoD BAH lookup. Learn more in our 2026 BAH guide.

Frequently asked questions

How much is BAH in Washington, DC Metro Area for an E-5?

In 2025, BAH for an E-5 in Washington, DC Metro Area is $2,952/month with dependents and $2,679/month without dependents.

How much is BAH for an E-7 in Washington, DC Metro Area?

An E-7 in Washington, DC Metro Area receives $3,579/month with dependents ($2,931 without) in 2025.

Is BAH in Washington, DC Metro Area taxable?

No. BAH is not subject to federal income tax, so you keep the full amount.

How BAH works in China Lake, CA

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the China Lake, CA Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, China Lake, CA's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

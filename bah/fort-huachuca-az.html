How BAH works in Fort Huachuca, AZ

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Fort Huachuca, AZ Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Fort Huachuca, AZ's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

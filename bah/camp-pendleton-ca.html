How BAH works in Camp Pendleton, CA

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Camp Pendleton, CA Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Camp Pendleton, CA's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

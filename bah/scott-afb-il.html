How BAH works in Scott AFB, IL

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Scott AFB, IL Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Scott AFB, IL's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

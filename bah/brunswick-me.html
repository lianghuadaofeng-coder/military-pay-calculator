How BAH works in Brunswick, ME

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Brunswick, ME Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Brunswick, ME's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

These are the official 2025 rates (the latest complete public dataset). 2026 BAH rose about 4.2% on average nationwide; for an exact current figure, enter your ZIP in the military pay calculator or check the DoD BAH lookup. Learn more in our 2026 BAH guide.

How BAH works in Fort Liberty / Pope, NC

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Fort Liberty / Pope, NC Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Fort Liberty / Pope, NC's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

How BAH works in Columbia / Jefferson City, MO

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Columbia / Jefferson City, MO Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Columbia / Jefferson City, MO's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

How BAH works in Indian Head Navordsta, MD

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Indian Head Navordsta, MD Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Indian Head Navordsta, MD's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.

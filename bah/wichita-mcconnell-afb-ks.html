How BAH works in Wichita / Mcconnell AFB, KS

BAH is set by the cost of rental housing in the Wichita / Mcconnell AFB, KS Military Housing Area, your pay grade, and your dependent status. It is paid monthly and is not taxed. Higher ranks and members with dependents receive more. Because it reflects local rents, Wichita / Mcconnell AFB, KS's rates differ from other locations — compare yours in the calculator.
